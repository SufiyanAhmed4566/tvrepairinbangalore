Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will you respond to my contact request? +

We typically respond within 15-30 minutes during business hours (8:00 AM - 9:00 PM). For WhatsApp messages, you'll usually get an instant response.

Do you charge for diagnosis? +

No, we provide free diagnosis for all TV issues. Our service charges are based on distance from our service center and are separate from repair costs.

What information should I have ready when contacting you? +

It's helpful to have your TV brand, model number (if known), a clear description of the problem, and your location ready when you contact us.

Can I send photos or videos of my TV issue? +

Yes! You can send photos or videos of your TV issue through WhatsApp. This helps our technicians better understand the problem before visiting.
