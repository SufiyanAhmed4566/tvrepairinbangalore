Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you book our TV repair services, we collect the following information:

  • Your name and contact details (phone number, email)
  • Your address for service delivery
  • TV model and issue description
  • Service history and preferences

1.2 Technical Information

We may collect technical information about your visit to our website, including:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device information

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision

We use your information to:

  • Schedule and provide TV repair services
  • Communicate about your service appointments
  • Process payments and send receipts
  • Provide customer support

Important: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is used solely for providing TV repair services and improving customer experience.

2.2 Communication

We may contact you to:

  • Confirm service appointments
  • Provide service updates
  • Send warranty information
  • Request feedback about our services

3. Data Protection and Security

3.1 Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Secure data storage systems
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Regular security assessments
  • Employee confidentiality agreements

3.2 Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the services you requested
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

4. Your Rights and Choices

4.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Request data portability

4.2 Communication Preferences

You can control how we communicate with you by:

  • Opting out of marketing communications
  • Setting your notification preferences
  • Contacting us to update your preferences

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in:

  • Payment processing
  • Customer support
  • Technical maintenance

Note: All third-party service providers are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your personal information.

6. Cookies and Tracking

6.1 Website Cookies

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Improve user experience
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Provide relevant content

6.2 Cookie Management

You can control cookie settings through your browser. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing notice where required by law
