üî¥ Cracked or Broken Screen

üü° Lines on Screen

Symptoms: Vertical/horizontal lines, colored lines, line patterns, permanent line display

Common Causes: T-Con board failure, panel damage, loose connections, internal circuit issues

üü† Black Spots or Patches

Symptoms: Dark spots, black patches, dead pixels, shadow areas on screen

Common Causes: Physical pressure damage, manufacturing defects, aging panel, impact points

üîµ Flickering or Flashing Screen

TV Screen Replacement FAQs

How do I know if my TV screen needs replacement? +

Your TV screen needs replacement if you see visible cracks, permanent lines, black spots, flickering, or physical damage affecting the display. Our technicians can diagnose whether screen replacement is necessary or if other repairs might solve the issue.

Do you use genuine screens for replacement? +

Yes, we prioritize using genuine, high-quality screens specifically matched to your TV model. We source screens from authorized suppliers or manufacturers to ensure perfect compatibility and optimal performance.

How long does screen replacement take? +

Most screen replacements are completed within 2-4 hours during the first visit. Complex replacements or rare screen models may require additional time for sourcing and installation. We maintain comprehensive inventory of common screen types.

Is it worth replacing TV screen or should I buy new TV? +

For mid-range and premium TVs, screen replacement is often more cost-effective than buying a new TV. We assess your specific situation and provide honest recommendations based on your TV's age, model, and replacement costs.

Do you replace OLED and QLED screens? +

Yes, we specialize in all screen technologies including OLED, QLED, NanoCell, and Mini-LED. Our technicians are trained in the specific handling and calibration requirements of premium screen technologies.

What's the warranty on screen replacement? +

We provide comprehensive 1-year warranty on all screen replacements, covering both the screen and labor. This ensures complete peace of mind and protection for your investment.
