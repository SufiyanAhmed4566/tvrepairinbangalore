TV Wall Mounting Service FAQs

How long does TV wall mounting take? +

Most standard TV wall mounting installations take 1-2 hours. Complex installations with full motion mounts or challenging wall types may take 2-3 hours. We work efficiently while ensuring perfect alignment and safety.

What's the ideal height for TV mounting? +

The ideal TV height is typically 42-48 inches from the floor to the center of the screen for seated viewing. Our technicians calculate the perfect height based on your seating arrangement and room layout for optimal comfort.

Can you mount TV on any type of wall? +

Yes, we mount TVs on all wall types including concrete, brick, drywall, and wood panels. We use appropriate anchors and mounting techniques specific to each wall type to ensure secure installation.

Do you provide wall mounts or should I buy? +

We can provide high-quality wall mounts suitable for your TV size and requirements, or we can install mounts you've purchased. We recommend mounts based on your TV specifications and viewing needs.

Is wall mounting safe for my TV? +

Yes, professional wall mounting is completely safe when done correctly. We use proper mounting hardware, follow manufacturer specifications, and ensure secure installation that can safely support your TV's weight.

Do you hide cables during installation? +

Yes, we provide professional cable management as part of our service. We use cable covers, in-wall solutions, and organization systems to create a clean, professional look with all cables neatly hidden.
