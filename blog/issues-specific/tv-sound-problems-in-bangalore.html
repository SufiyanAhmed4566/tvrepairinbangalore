TV Sound Problems Repair FAQs

Why is there no sound from my TV? +

Complete audio loss is usually caused by audio board failure, speaker connection issues, main board problems, or software glitches. Our technicians use specialized audio testing equipment to diagnose the exact cause and implement permanent solutions.

My TV sound is distorted - what's wrong? +

Distorted sound typically indicates damaged speakers, audio amplifier issues, loose connections, or capacitor problems. We test each component systematically to identify the root cause and restore clear audio quality.

Why does my sound keep cutting in and out? +

Intermittent sound is usually caused by loose wiring, connection issues, audio board component failure, or thermal problems. Our technicians identify the specific cause and implement reliable fixes to ensure consistent audio performance.

Can HDMI ARC sound issues be fixed? +

Yes, most HDMI ARC issues are repairable. Common solutions include port repair, configuration adjustments, software updates, or component replacement. We specialize in HDMI audio system troubleshooting for all major TV brands.

How long do speaker repairs last? +

With genuine parts and professional installation, speaker repairs typically last 4-6 years or more. Our 1-year warranty ensures complete peace of mind, and we use high-quality components designed for long-term reliability.

Do you repair Dolby Atmos systems? +

Yes, we specialize in premium audio systems including Dolby Atmos, DTS:X, and other immersive audio technologies. Our technicians are trained in advanced audio calibration and system troubleshooting for optimal sound performance.
