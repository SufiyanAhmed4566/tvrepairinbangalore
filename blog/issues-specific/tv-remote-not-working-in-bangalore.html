TV Remote Repair FAQs

Why is my TV remote not working at all? +

Complete remote failure is usually caused by dead batteries, battery contact corrosion, internal circuit board failure, or IR transmitter issues. Our technicians use specialized equipment to diagnose the exact cause and provide permanent solutions.

Some buttons work but others don't - what's wrong? +

This indicates worn-out button contacts, dirty circuit board, carbon pad deterioration, or internal connection issues. We clean and repair the circuit board or replace specific button mechanisms to restore full functionality.

My remote works intermittently - why? +

Intermittent operation is typically caused by loose battery connections, damaged IR LED, circuit board cracks, or power supply instability. Our technicians identify the root cause and implement permanent fixes.

Can water damage to remote be fixed? +

Yes, most water-damaged remotes can be repaired if addressed promptly. We perform thorough cleaning, corrosion removal, and component replacement to restore functionality. Success rates are high when repairs are done quickly.

How long do remote repairs last? +

With genuine parts and professional repair, remote controls typically last 3-5 years or more. Our 1-year warranty ensures complete peace of mind, and we use high-quality components designed for long-term reliability.

Do you repair Bluetooth and smart remotes? +

Yes, we specialize in all types of remotes including Bluetooth, WiFi, voice control, and smart remotes. Our technicians are trained in modern remote technologies and can handle pairing issues, connectivity problems, and advanced feature repairs.
