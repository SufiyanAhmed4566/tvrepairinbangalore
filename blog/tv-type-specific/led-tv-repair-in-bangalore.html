LED TV Repair FAQs

Why is my LED TV screen completely dark but I can hear sound? +

This indicates a complete LED backlight failure, typically caused by LED strip issues, backlight driver board problems, or power supply failure to the LED system. Our technicians diagnose the exact cause and restore proper backlight functionality.

Can LED TV backlight problems be fixed permanently? +

Yes, most LED TV backlight issues can be permanently resolved through LED strip replacement, driver board repair, or complete backlight system overhaul. With genuine parts and professional installation, the repair typically lasts for years.

How long does LED TV repair take? +

Most LED TV repairs are completed within 2-4 hours during the first visit. Complex repairs requiring special LED components may take 24-48 hours. We maintain comprehensive inventory of common LED TV parts for quick service.

Is it worth repairing LED TV or should I buy new? +

In most cases, LED TV repair is significantly more cost-effective than buying a new TV, especially for larger screen sizes and premium models. We assess your specific situation and provide honest recommendations based on repair feasibility and cost.

Do you repair smart LED TV features and connectivity? +

Yes, we specialize in repairing all smart TV features including Wi-Fi connectivity, app functionality, software updates, and streaming issues on LED TVs. Our technicians are trained in both hardware and software troubleshooting.

What's the lifespan of repaired LED TV? +

With genuine parts and professional installation, repaired LED TVs typically last 5-7 years or more. Our 1-year warranty ensures complete peace of mind for all LED TV repairs and components.
