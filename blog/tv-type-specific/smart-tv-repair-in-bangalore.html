Smart TV Repair FAQs

Why is my Smart TV stuck on the logo screen? +

This is usually caused by corrupted system files, failed software updates, or hardware issues. Our technicians perform system diagnostics to identify the exact cause and restore proper boot functionality through software reinstallation or hardware repair.

Can Smart TV software issues be fixed permanently? +

Yes, most Smart TV software problems can be permanently resolved through system reinstallation, firmware updates, or hardware replacement when necessary. We provide comprehensive solutions including preventive measures for long-term stability.

How long does Smart TV repair take? +

Most Smart TV software repairs are completed within 1-2 hours. Hardware-related issues may take 2-4 hours. We maintain comprehensive tools and software for efficient Smart TV repair across all major brands and operating systems.

Is it worth repairing Smart TV or should I buy new? +

In most cases, Smart TV repair is significantly more cost-effective than buying a new TV, especially for premium models with good hardware. We assess your specific situation and provide honest recommendations based on repair feasibility and long-term value.

Do you repair voice control and smart features? +

Yes, we specialize in voice control systems (Google Assistant, Alexa, Bixby) and smart feature repair including microphone issues, voice recognition problems, and integration troubleshooting with smart home devices and ecosystems.

What's the lifespan of repaired Smart TV? +

With professional repair and proper maintenance, Smart TVs typically last 4-6 years or more. Our 1-year warranty ensures complete peace of mind for all Smart TV repairs, covering both software and hardware components.
